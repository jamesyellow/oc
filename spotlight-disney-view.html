DOWNTOWN DISNEY

Too broke for Disneyland? Or don't have time to hang out somewhere for the whole day? One thing I love about Downtown Disney is how you can just chill there for an hour or two. The best part? You get to experience the magic of Disney without having to enter the theme park. Especially fun for holiday seasons, late night summer hangouts, and casual leisure time with loved ones.
